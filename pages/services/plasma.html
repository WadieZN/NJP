Platelet Rich Plasma Injections

Joint regeneration therapy, also called stem cell therapy for joints, is a process that uses your body’s own healing capability to combat injuries and diseases to your joints. By using your own stem cells from your bone marrow or adipose fat tissue, regenerative cellular therapy encourages renewed healthy cell growth. Stem cells injected into the problem joint become bone, tendon or soft tissue as needed for joint regeneration therapy that truly heals.
