Welcome to the Practice of Douglas Manganelli, MD and Shane Huch, MD

When you’re looking for a way to end your pain for good, Center for Regenerative Therapy and Pain Management can help. Dr. Managanelli and Dr. Huch are pain management doctors who provides personalized consultations to help diagnose and treat whatever injury or condition is causing your pain, with a special focus on spine, joint, and headache pain.

Our Brick, NJ facility offers state-of-the-art pain treatments, including platelet-rich plasma therapy (PRP), which has helped many successful athletes to heal quickly. We also specialize in comprehensive non-surgical pain management treatments including injections, BOTOX® for headaches, and medication management, as well as other services such as genetic testing, headache evaluation, and bone health testing. Dr. Douglas Manganelli, MD

Dr. Douglas Manganelli is certified by the American Board of Anesthesiology and the American Board of Pain Medicine. He is a graduate of Rutgers University and finished his schooling at St. George’s University School of Medicine. He completed his anesthesiology residency at both St. Luke’s-Roosevelt Hospital and St. Joseph’s Hospital and also completed a family practice residency at St. Mary’s Hospital. He is a past chairman for the Department of Anesthesia at Jersey Shore Medical Center.

Dr. Douglas Manganelli is a founding partner in Center for Regenerative Therapy and Pain Management. He is a member of the American Society of Anesthesiologists and the New Jersey State Society of Anesthesiologists.

Douglas Manganelli M.D. is a Board-Certified Physician in Anesthesia and Pain Management.
